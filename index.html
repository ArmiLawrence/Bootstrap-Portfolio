About Me

I was born in Manila, Philippines and I enjoyed school, movies, TV, and most of all, playing outside during our hot summers.

I graduated from the University of California, Berkeley many moons ago, with a major in History and minor in City and Regional Planning. Need for food and shelter hindered my lifelong goal of becoming a hippie, so I got into maintenance renewals sales. From there, I got into working with data and learned the fun world of Microsoft SQL stack and have been working with data for the last 6 years. I graduated from the University of Denver's Full Stack Coding Boot Camp. Feel free to browse through my portfolio, and I aim to add more as I create apps for my personal and professional interests. I enjoy learning new technologies and programming languages.

As hobbies, I run (very slowly) and practice kickboxing and karate. I believe that martial arts is a good way to improve one's character. My "normal" people activities are watching new movies on TV or theater, and anime in VRV. I have no kids, no pets, but don't mind playing with those beings once in a while.

Fun Fact: I have a twin sister, she is my clone.
